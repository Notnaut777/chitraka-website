About Chitraka

Discover the art of visual storytelling

Our Vision

Chitraka is the premier photography and cinematography club of IIT Jammu, dedicated to fostering creativity and technical excellence in visual arts. We believe that every frame tells a story, and every story deserves to be told beautifully.

What We Do

From capturing the vibrant campus life to creating compelling short films, our club serves as a creative hub for students passionate about visual storytelling. We organize workshops, competitions, and collaborative projects that push the boundaries of conventional photography and cinematography.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for budding photographers and filmmakers to hone their skills, share their vision, and create impactful visual content that resonates with audiences. We aim to bridge the gap between technical knowledge and artistic expression.

Our Activities

Explore what makes Chitraka unique

Photography Workshops

Learn from industry professionals and master the art of capturing perfect moments. From basic camera operations to advanced techniques.

Film Making

Create compelling short films, documentaries, and promotional videos. Learn scriptwriting, directing, and post-production techniques.

Competitions

Participate in inter-college competitions and showcase your talent. Win exciting prizes and gain recognition for your work.

Photo Exhibitions

Display your best work in our regular exhibitions. Get feedback from peers and industry experts to improve your craft.

Collaborative Projects

Work on exciting projects with fellow members. From campus events coverage to creative storytelling initiatives.

Technical Sessions
