Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Chitraka Photography & Cinematography Club

General Information

Chitraka is the official photography and cinematography club of IIT Jammu. It aims to promote visual storytelling through photography, videography, and creative media among students.

Any student of IIT Jammu with an interest in photography, filmmaking, editing, or creative media is welcome to join the club.

You can become a member by filling out the registration form circulated at the beginning of each semester or by contacting the club coordinators directly.

No prior experience or professional skills are required. Enthusiasm, curiosity, and a willingness to learn are all you need.

No, membership is completely free of cost for all IIT Jammu students.

Currently, membership is limited to IIT Jammu students. However, we welcome collaborations and external contributions for special projects.

Activities & Events

Chitraka organizes workshops, photo walks, filmmaking contests, exhibitions, event coverages, editing bootcamps, and participation in inter-college competitions.

Yes, the club regularly conducts workshops on photography basics, advanced camera handling, post-processing, and video editing.

Absolutely! Chitraka encourages beginners and provides mentorship and learning opportunities for everyone.

We typically have weekly or bi-weekly meetings, and events are organized throughout the semester based on the calendar.

Yes, we organize regular internal competitions and also encourage participation in national-level photography and filmmaking contests.

Yes, we select and train members to represent the club and the institute in various inter-IIT and national-level photography and filmmaking contests.

Equipment & Resources

The club uses DSLR cameras, tripods, lenses, microphones, and other gear provided by the institute. Members can also use their own equipment.

Yes, members can borrow equipment for club-related projects or events with prior permission and proper responsibility.

We primarily use Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and Canva. Beginners are trained in these tools during workshops.

Even without a camera, you can contribute through scriptwriting, editing, storyboarding, voiceovers, and managing social media or events.

Showcasing Work & Recognition

You can showcase your work through our social media handles, club exhibitions, or during institute events where we display creative content.

Yes, active members receive participation certificates, recognition during institute events, and opportunities to lead major projects.

You can find our past work on our social media platforms, website gallery, and during annual campus exhibitions.

Management & Contact

Chitraka is managed by a team of student coordinators under the guidance of a faculty advisor. Positions are filled through annual elections or nominations.

You can stay updated by joining our official WhatsApp group, checking our notice boards, or following us on Instagram (@chitraka_iitjammu).

Yes! Follow us on Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook – just search for "Chitraka IIT Jammu."

You can reach out via email (chitraka@iitjammu.ac.in), message us on Instagram, or talk to any of the student coordinators directly.

Collaborations & Alumni

Yes, we actively collaborate with cultural and technical clubs within IIT Jammu and occasionally with clubs from other IITs and institutions.

Alumni can support by mentoring, sharing industry experience, reviewing student portfolios, or participating in alumni projects.
